LAN HA BAY CRUISES

Located to the East of Cat ba Islands, the South of Halong Bay, Lan Ha Bay is a part of Gulf of Tonkin belonged to Hai Phong City. With around 7000ha widespread over the sea, owning almost similar geological features to Halong Bay, Lan Ha Bay atracts tourists by unspoiled landscape which is less touristic than Halong Bay’s destinations.


Being a continuous portion of mighty karst formation from Halong Bay, Lan Ha Bay possesses approximately 400 islands and islets situating in the area. Many islands here have splitted the sea to form natural lagoons which is totally isolated from the outside world. Moreover, as a part of Cat Ba Archipelago – a UNESCO World Biosphere Reserve, Lan Ha Bay has pristine nature, untouched areas and a diverse ecosystem which will surely allure anyone prefererring a completely relaxing and peaceful trip.

HOW TO GET LAN HA BAY

Almost Lan Ha Bay Cruises will depart from Got Ferry in Cat Hai Town, Hai Phong City. Nowadays, several new ships have offered new routes which depart from Tuan Chau Marina, Halong City. There are also two ways to reach Got Ferry or Tuan Chau Marina, using shuttle bus from cruise or using private transfer service.

* If you choose shuttle bus from cruise:

Your cruise departs from Got Ferry: Normally, any cruise package will include transfer cost with roundtrip shuttle bus transfer Hanoi – Got Ferry – Hanoi, which means you will use the bus with other guests as well. All cruise programs in Lan Ha Bay departing from Got Ferry will start to pick up guests from Hanoi Old Quarter (at around 7:15 – 7:45 AM for day cruises and around 9:00 – 9:30 AM for overnight cruises) with the route of Highway Hanoi – Hai Phong then following Tan Vu- Lach Huyen Bridge to reach Got Ferry in Hai Phong City for around 2 hours driving.

Your cruise departs from Tuan Chau Marina: Almost cruise programs in Lan Ha Bay, which depart from Tuan Chau Marina, will start to pick up guests from Hanoi Old Quarter (at around 7:00 – 7:30 AM for day cruises and around 9:00 – 9:15 AM for overnight cruises) then transfer to Tuan Chau Marina in Halong Bay with the route of Highway Hanoi – Hai Phong – Ha Long for around 2.5 hours driving.

Note: In case you’re staying out of Hanoi Old Quarter Area, you’d better handle youself a transfer helping you arrive scheduled pick-up point in Old Quarter on time.

* If you prefer private transfer service:

You can easily rent a private car with private driver following to your schedule from vendors around Old Quarter or agents helping you book cruise package. Driving time is also around 2 to 2.5 hours driving but private transfer will offer privacy to you only.

LAN HA BAY CRUISE ITINERARIES

Lan Ha Bay Cruise 2 Days 1 Night Itinerary:

Day 1:

Shuttle bus/ private car start to pick up guests from Hanoi the transfer to Cruise Waiting Lounges in Got Ferry, Hai Phong City or Tuan Chau Marina, Halong Bay. Check-in and embark the cruise.

Note: If cruise route departs from Got Ferry, guests will get on tender then transfer for approx 20 minutes to reach the main ship. In case cruise route departs from Tuan Chau Marina, guests will get on speedboat with transfer time is around 30 minutes to get on the main ship mooring in Gia Luan Area (Hai Phong City).

Welcome onboard. Receive room keys. Have lunch at the cruise’s restaurant and take a rest.

Join main interesting activities in the afternoon, which could be optional depending on each cruise’s program such as: kayaking in allowed untouched areas, visiting Trung Trang Cave in Cat Ba Island.

End of main activity, back to the main ship, relax in room or at the sundeck to see the stunning sunset in Lan Ha Bay then join traditional cooking class.

Dinner is served in restaurant, which could be set menu or BBQ buffet. Freely relax at the sundeck; in cruise bar and enjoy music or experience squid fishing. End of day 1.

Day 2:

Wake up early and witness breathtaking sunrise in beautiful Lan Ha bay then participate in Tai Chi Session – a traditional Asian morning exercise.

Having light breakfast and join main interesting activities in the morning, which could be optional depending on each cruise’s program such as: visiting Dark – Bright Cave by kayaking/ rowed boat or kayaking to explore pristine Lan Ha Bay.

Back to the main ship, take a short rest and ready for check-out.

Having a brunch onboard before coming back the mainland, settle payment for extra service used onboard: beverage or spa treatment, etc.

Disembark the cruise, back to Waiting Lounge in Got Ferry or Tuan Chau Marina, take back the luggage, get on the bus/ private car then back to Hanoi. End of Lan Ha Bay 2 days 1 night cruise program.

Lan Ha Bay Day Cruise Itinerary:

Shuttle bus/ private car start to pick up guests from Hanoi the transfer to Cruise Waiting Lounges in Got Ferry, Hai Phong City or Tuan Chau Marina, Halong Bay. Revise guests’s information and embark the cruise.

Welcome onboard. Have lunch at the cruise’s restaurant and take a rest.

Join main interesting activities on day boat including exploring Dark – Bright Cave by rowed boat then enjoying kayaking and swimming at natural lagoons in Lan Ha Bay.

Back to the day boat and join Vietnam traditional cruisine cooking demonstration. Several day cruises will offer additional activities such as visiting floating houses in the Bay or sunset party onboard.

Day boat turns back the mainland at around 5:30 PM. Get on the shuttle bus/ private car for going back to Hanoi and expected time for reaching city centre is around 7:30 – 8:00 PM.
