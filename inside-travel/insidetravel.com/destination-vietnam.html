The Land of Timeless Charm

Welcome to Vietnam the land of timeless charm. Being favored by nature with long coastal line: stretching from stunning Halong Bay UNESCO World Natural Heritage Site, charming Hoi An Danang, frenetic Nha Trang to unique Mekong Delta region, Vietnam is praised as the paradise for relaxing vacations in Southeast Asia.

EXPLORE OUR Vietnam

Located in the heart of South East Asia, Vietnam has been endowed with diverse types of terrain, beautiful landscapes, famous historical sites and rich culture of ethnic minorities, which make it become one of the most attractive destinations in the world. Most travelers to Vietnam are always inspired from the countrys stunning natural beauty, from the green fields in the north, pristine beaches along the country, to the fascinating bustle and hustle of the Mekong Delta in the south.

Vietnam is also the house of many historic attractions, old pagodas and temples, which attracts a large number of tourists coming to explore.

Rarely has a country changed so much over a short time as Vietnam. Since the slaughter and savagery of the American War, the country has been buoyant with much hope. It is on the move with easy access, upgraded roads, developed economics and especially, tourism is getting more and more developing. Vietnam is the country of huge and diverse tourism potential with the famous relics, landscapes, National Parks and cultural heritages. Vietnam has 31 national parks with some well-known as Phong Nha Ke Bang, Ba Be Lake, Cat Ba, Con Dao, Cuc Phuong, Phu Quoc, Tam Dao, etc

The country is also the safest destination where you can witness the World Heritage Sites including Central Sector of the Imperial Citadel of Thang Long Hanoi, My Son Sanctuary, Citadel of the Ho Dynasty, Hoi An Ancient Town, Complex of Hue Monuments and Ha Long Bay.

Vietnam has owned 21 national tourist areas that are recognized by the state as Sa Pa, Perfume Pagoda, Ba Be, Kim Lien (Nghe An), Hoi An, Da Lat, Phan Thiet Mui Ne, Con Dao, Phu Quoc, Cu Chi Tunnels and Ho Chi Minh trail, Khau Pha Pass known for terraced rice paddies, Moc Chau Plateau with pristine white flowers fields, Trang An Eco-Tourism Complex which is considered as Halong Bay on Land

With the long coastline stretching along the country, it's no surprise that Vietnam has a large number of beautiful beaches with sparkling white sand and clear blue water. Some of them are: Tra Co in Mong Cai, a unspoiled and unpolluted beach with long coastline, sapphire water, wide waves and romantic sunsets; Cua Dai beach, one of the 25 most beautiful beaches on the planet, has white sand, blue water, windy sea and covered by lush coconuts trees. Other beaches tourists cant ignore these following beaches including Cat Ba, Do Son, My Khe, Cua Lo, Lang Co, Nha Trang, etc

Besides, Vietnam is one of the countries having stable conditions of security, politics which attracts a lot of foreign visitors. Moreover, Vietnamese people are known for the hospitality, friendliness, warmth and enthusiasm. Whoever coming to Vietnam is impressed not only by world-renowned destinations but also by Vietnamese people and so Vietnam is becoming an ideal stop for travel lovers.
