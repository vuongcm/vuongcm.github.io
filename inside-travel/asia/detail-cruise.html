Itineraries & Routes

2

President Cruise 2 days 1 night

Price from $150 $214 pp

Day 1: Hanoi - Hai Phong - Trung Trang cave

Transfer:
Drive from Water Puppet Show to Local Restaurant for Dinner and back to the hotel (2 km / way) Walk from your hotel to Water Puppet Theater (500m - 900m) Transfer from Hanoi Airport to hotel in the Old Quarter (40km)
Activities:

Driving to Hai Phong province, Arrive in Got Port, Check in Orchid Cruise, Welcome Drink, Having lunch while enjoying the natural beauty of bays, Visiting Trung Trang cave, Participating in traditional cooking class, Enjoying night activities

09h10 You will be collected from Hanoi Old Quarter to take the new highway to Got Ferry Terminal – Cat Hai Town, Haiphong City where we arrive at around 11h30. The passengers have a chance to experience on longest and newest bridge in South East Asia area. You can take special tea at Orchid Cruise’s lounge by warm welcome.

12h15 Transfer by tender to the ORCHID CRUISE.

12h30 We welcome our guests onboard the ORCHID CRUISE and then check in, enjoy Welcome Drink, receive cruise briefing, safety instructions and check in. Start cruising and explore Halong Bay - one of the New Seven Wonders of Nature.

13h00 ORCHID CRUISE proceeds to Da Chong islet, where you can see a lighthouse built by the French over 100 years ago. Have International Buffet Lunch while passing via the islets of Con Vit area. These amazing limestone karst formations rise up out of the sea in an impressive manner. This area is untouched, as not many cruise ships use the route via the Cat Ba Island - Thoi Quyt area - Coc Ngoi islet - through Gia Luan area where is belong to Cat Ba National Park.

15h00 We progress to Trung Trang cave by our bus on Cat Ba island which is located in the middle of Cat Ba National Park with Kim Giao forest around the cave.

Trung Trang Valley is the largest valley on Cat Ba Island. The valley covers an area of approximately 300 hectares, has a depth of 10m to 30m and is around 15km in the middle of Cat Ba National Park. Trung Trang Cave measures 300m lengthwise through the mountains. The cave was carved by nature for more than 2000 years. The cave has many stalactites that take on various shapes that will awe and inspire visitors to use their imagination.

17h00 Orchid Cruise gets in the less populated area at Tra Bau where is located in Lan Ha Bay. You will enjoy the green emerald and cool water by swimming

17h45 Returning ORCHID CRUISE, you can watch the magnificent sunset over the Gulf of Tonkin. Onboard you may join a traditional cooking class. Our chef will instruct you in the making of some Vietnam dishes. In the early evening the boat has its Happy Hour deal

Option: Massage service available. Please book with Cruise Manager directly (please see massage menu)

19h30 Sumptuous Candlelight Deluxe Dinner Set Menu is served. Enjoy delicious local food prepared and served for you by our wonderful onboard catering staff. This is a delightful meal in an intimate romantic setting

21h30 Enjoy the landscape of the bay at night up on the top deck. Relax whilst enjoying drinks from the bar. Try your hand at night squid fishing, watching a movie in the restaurant. Overnight on board.

Option: Massage service available. Please book with Cruise Manager directly (please see massage menu)

23h00 End of Day One program

Signature Michellin Menu

14

Spacious boat with complete privacy

The five-deck steel vessel measures 86 meters in length, 13.9 meters in width and 13 meters in height, which makes President Cruise the largest overnight ship in the legendary Halong Bay. Inside the cruise, the sublime assortment of onboard recreations, premium facilities, and artfully decorated cabins will definitely dazzle all passengers. Besides the spacious twin-sundecks and the fascinating outdoor Jacuzzi on the third deck, President Cruise also provides private balconies or terraces in all cabins that let passengers soak in the beauty of Halong Bay without any distraction.

14

Unique Evening Entertainment with Brazilian Jazz Band

President Cruise does not only offer first-rate service but also make your trip even more incredible with an incredible Latin-America jazz duo to end a great night out in the graceful piano bar. Inspired by the classic ambiance of a 1920s New York jazz café, President Cruise is the only cruise ship offering sophisticated international evening entertainment in Halong Bay. Let’s imagine how wonderful it is to enjoy the most classic Jazz hits performed by truly professional musicians in the center of World Heritage Site.

Lan Ha Bay

Ao Ech Area

Kayaking Cultural

Located on the eastern side of Cat Ba Island, Lan Ha Bay is a very calm bay with about 400 large and small islands and 139 small golden sand beaches creating a beautiful natural picture. Ao Ech Area is one of the best attractions on Lan Ha Bay which is best known for its pristine and tranquil beauty. The whole area is an unspoiled waterscape created by stone mountains covering around a crystal clear water and small untouched sandy beaches.

Ao Ech is also an ideal place for water sports, especially kayaking activity. Choosing a kayak to explore Lan Ha Bay will bring new experiences that cruise ships cannot have. Passing through small islands and caves, kayak gives you a close look to the foot of the rocky mountains and watching the numerous vivid scenes of the sea world under the clear blue water.
