With Belmond’s exclusive selection of inspiring activities, you’ll find endless things to do in Asia. Browse through our top recommendations below

Bagan

The Magic of Bagan

Meet your guide for a tour of Old Bagan by horse and cart. Soak up the sights and sounds of this fascinating city from a very authentic vantage point.
Luang Prabang

Tak Bat & Sunrise Cruise

As dawn approaches, head into town to gift food to a procession of local monks in their vibrant orange robes. Then enjoy a magical sunrise breakfast cruise along the Mekong River.
Yangon

First Light at Shwedagon

The most iconic sight of Yangon, visit Shwedagon Pagoda at sunrise to see it in truly magical light. Led by a private guide, join locals and make an offering to your planetary post.
Angkor

Sunrise over Angkor Wat

Experience Angkor’s mystical jungle in blissful quiet on a dawn walk. Tread a tranquil route, away from the crowds, reaching Angkor Wat in time to watch the sun rise over the majestic temple.
Koh Samui

Treasures of Koh Samui

Explore the idyllic waters off the coast of Koh Samui. Head to the Ang Thong National Marine Park, a fascinating archipelago of over 40 islands across the Central Gulf Coast of Southern Thailand.
Kanchanburi

Local Flavours

Alight at Kanchanaburi, Thailand to explore the wet market, a rice noodle house and attend a cooking class while rafting down the river.
