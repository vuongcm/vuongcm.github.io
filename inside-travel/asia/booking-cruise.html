25’ Scenic Flight Over Halong Bay

It is said that sightseeing seaplanes are an amazing and exciting way to explore the natural beauty of Halong Bay from the air and see the magnificent views of limestones and breathtaking landsapes of the UNESCO World Heritage. Taking off and landing on the water is a unique experience you should never miss out!

Departure & Arrival Points

Tuan Chau Marina: No T7 Tuan Chau Commercial Urban Area, Tuan Chau Ward, Ha Long City, Quang Ninh Province, Viet Nam.

Features

- Flight Path: Tuan Chau – Cong Do - Bo Hon – Dau Be – Tuan Chau

- The seaplanes take off from the Tuan Chau Marina and fly over Halong Bay for 25 minutes.

- In order to make sure the flight fits your schedule, you are advised to use private cars for more mobility.

- At the beginning of the trip, you will be transferred to No T7 Tuan Chau Commercial Urban Area, Tuan Chau Ward, Ha Long City to take the flight. Hanoi Foodie Tour

Hanoi Foodie tour takes hungry souls from around the world on Hanoi street food that delight and excite the senses. Accompanied by one of our experienced local guide, you will learn about Hanoi’s famously food-obsessed culture and savor its best-loved dishes at hidden-away neighborhood gems.

Group size: from 2 – 10 pax.

Departure time: from 17:00 and ends at 18:30.

Meeting point: TBA.

The best way to approach a culture is to explore its cuisine. This tour helps you discover the ancient Hanoi old quarter throught tasting its authentic local street food From Nom (Vietnamese salad with beef), Pho (Beef noodle), Bun Cha (Vietnamese grilled pork with vemicelli) and end up with Ca Phe Trung - a blend of coffee and egg whites, folded with sugar. Seasonal Mixed Fruits is an option for who don’t “dare” to give Egg coffee a shot.


Eat like a local! Discover Hanoi foodies’ best loved dishes in neighborhood locales.

Lan Ha Bay

Ao Ech Area

Kayaking Cultural

Located on the eastern side of Cat Ba Island, Lan Ha Bay is a very calm bay with about 400 large and small islands and 139 small golden sand beaches creating a beautiful natural picture. Ao Ech Area is one of the best attractions on Lan Ha Bay which is best known for its pristine and tranquil beauty. The whole area is an unspoiled waterscape created by stone mountains covering around a crystal clear water and small untouched sandy beaches.

Ao Ech is also an ideal place for water sports, especially kayaking activity. Choosing a kayak to explore Lan Ha Bay will bring new experiences that cruise ships cannot have. Passing through small islands and caves, kayak gives you a close look to the foot of the rocky mountains and watching the numerous vivid scenes of the sea world under the clear blue water.
