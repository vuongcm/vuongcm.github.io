Vietnam, the beautiful S-shaped country in the Indochina Peninsula, is known to be an enchanting destination in Southeast Asia by its timeless beauty of nature, interesting history, and intriguing culture. To many travellers, Vietnam has plentiful of amazing things to explore. Each destination in this country is packed with charming sceneries as well as various local experiences that will definitely blow your mind away. Vietnam is a should-not-miss stop in Asia where you can encounter the captivating blend of both the old and the new, of the Eastern and the Western.
